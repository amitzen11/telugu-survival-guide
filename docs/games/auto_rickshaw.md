## Tips for Success
- **Use the Suffixes:** Suffixes like `-andi` (respectful) and `-ra` (casual inquiry) matter drastically depending on who you are talking to.
- **English Tax:** Reverting to pure English phrases will immediately drop your respect score—and likely raise your fare!
- **Play to the Personality:** Different drivers want different things. Does your driver want respectful distance, or do they want to hear local slang?
