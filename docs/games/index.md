### Why play?
Rote memorization only gets you so far. When you're standing at the Secunderabad railway station and an auto driver quotes you 500 rupees for a 3km ride, you need muscle memory.

These interactive scenarios let you safely practice the **exact phrases** you'll need under pressure.
