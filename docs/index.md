# Welcome to Telugu Survival Guide

Namaskaram! Welcome to your essential guide for surviving and thriving in Hyderabad using Telugu.

## The Linguistic Landscape of Hyderabad
Hyderabad is a vibrant city where Telugu, Hindi, and English intertwine. While English and Hindi are widely understood, especially in professional settings, speaking even a little Telugu can significantly enhance your experience, foster deeper connections, and make daily life smoother. It's not just about communication; it's about embracing the local culture.

## 🎯 Our Mission
To bridge the gap between "I don't speak Telugu" and confident daily interactions. We provide practical, street-ready phrases, cultural nuances, and survival vocabulary to help you navigate Hyderabad with ease.

## 👥 Who Is This For?
Anyone who has recently moved to Hyderabad for work, speaks English or Hindi, and wants to connect with locals, order food, negotiate with auto drivers, and build relationships without feeling like a complete outsider.
